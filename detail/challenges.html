Finclusion: MSME Credit Scoring

Develop alternative credit scoring models using non-traditional data sources for micro, small, and medium enterprises to improve financial inclusion.

Challenge Description

Traditional credit scoring models often exclude micro, small, and medium enterprises (MSMEs) from accessing formal financial services due to lack of sufficient credit history and traditional financial data. This challenge aims to develop innovative credit scoring solutions that leverage alternative data sources to assess creditworthiness of MSMEs.

We are looking for solutions that can:

  • Utilize non-traditional data sources such as mobile money transactions, utility payments, social media activity, and business performance indicators
  • Provide real-time credit scoring capabilities with high accuracy
  • Integrate seamlessly with existing banking systems
  • Ensure compliance with local financial regulations and data privacy laws
  • Support multiple languages and local business contexts

The winning solution will be piloted with Unity Bank's MSME customers and potentially scaled across the Malaysian banking sector.

Requirements

Technical Requirements

  • Machine learning models with explainable AI capabilities
  • API integration for real-time scoring
  • Cloud-based deployment with 99.9% uptime
  • Data encryption and secure data handling
  • Scalable architecture supporting 100,000+ concurrent users

Data Requirements

  • Integration with mobile money platforms (GrabPay, Touch 'n Go, etc.)
  • Utility payment data (electricity, water, internet)
  • Social media and digital footprint analysis
  • Business registration and licensing data
  • Alternative financial transaction data
