About Us
Building Malaysia’s Open Innovation Future

MyInnovationX is Malaysia’s premier open innovation platform — connecting corporates, startups, universities, government agencies, and innovators to co-create solutions for real-world challenges. We believe every challenge is an opportunity, and every innovator has the potential to make an impact.

Build Ecosystems

Building Tomorrow's Solutions Today

Connecting visionaries across industries to create meaningful impact

Our Story

Founded with a vision to transform how innovation happens in Southeast Asia, MyInnovationX emerged from the recognition that the region's incredible potential was being held back by fragmented ecosystems and limited collaboration opportunities.

We started as a small team of passionate innovators who believed that the future of Southeast Asia lies in its ability to harness technology and innovation to solve real-world challenges. Today, we've grown into a comprehensive platform that connects over 500+ innovators, 30+ successful projects, and 200+ marketplace listings.

Operated by Altomate Corporate Services Sdn. Bhd., we're committed to building bridges between different stakeholders in the innovation ecosystem, making it easier for great ideas to find the resources, partners, and support they need to succeed.
